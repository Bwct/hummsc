Teresa Hanging Bridge

Located in Brgy. Prinza, the Teresa Hanging Bridge is an iconic landmark in the town. It provides a unique experience of crossing a bridge suspended over a river, offering beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.

Barangay Dulumbayan

Dulumbayan is a barangay in the municipality of Teresa, in the province of Rizal. Its population as determined by the 2020 Census was 9,295. This represented 14.51% of the total population of Teresa.
